About SCRAPBOTS:
SCRAPBOTS is a simple battling game which allows players to create robots using Scrap, battle other users' robots and collect Scrap as a reward and to continuesly improve their robots and to improve their rank in the leaderboards

Robots:
Users have a choice of creating 3 types of robots: Aerial, Bipedal and Wheeled which all have certain advantage over the other when first created

Battles:
Users can anounce that they are open to battle by activiating the matchmaking button in their profile page and have a choice of battling with a team of 1,3 and 5 robots.
